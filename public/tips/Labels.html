Label Syntax

Labels must consist of one to six alphanumeric characters, with the first character being either alphabetic or one of the special characters ? or @. Each label must be followed by a colon : to indicate that it is a label. There should be no space between the word and the : character. Additionally, a label can only be defined once in the program, and it must be unique. 

LABEL: MVI A, 0AH; LOAD 0AH INTO REGISTER A
